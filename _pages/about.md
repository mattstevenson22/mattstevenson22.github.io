Hello, I'm Matt.

I recently graduated from Imperial College London with an MEng in Electronic and Information Engineering. I am interested in studying emergence and complexity through the lens of information theory. 

I have a wide variety of other interests, most notably in literature, history (particularly Anglo-Saxon England), and architecture. More practically, I enjoy walking in the English countryside (especially North Yorkshire) and cooking (especially Cantonese food). I admire the polymaths of the past and hope to live my life in somewhat of the same vein.
